Upcoming Events

While the list in the talks section of my blog covers past presentations, this page should, if I keep it up to date, list my confirmed upcoming events.

22-23 Sep: London - Just passing through. Always open to offers of drinks (wine or coffee are both acceptable).

25-28 Sep: San Francisco - For pre-JavaOne events & meetups.

29 Sep - 3 Oct: JavaOne - presenting “HTML5/AngularJS/Groovy/Java and MongoDB Together: What Could Possibly Go Wrong?“, and unconfirmed for a second session. I love meeting people at JavaOne, come find me and say hello - I often hang out in the Taylor St Cafe area. Or in a bar. Preferably with free wine.

14 Oct: JAX London - presenting the Angular/Java/MongoDB demo, at 5:45pm. I’ll also be hanging out, as usual, on the community night, although I’m not sure what date that is yet.

16 Oct: GeekoutUK, London - “Pick the right tools to improve your productivity”, a variation on the demo to emphasize the productivity aspects. I’ll also be around for the community night.

13-16 Oct: With JAX and GeekoutUK this week, I’ll be in London for the whole week, so if you want me for a London-based event, this is the week to put it in!

6 Nov: GOTO Berlin - where I’m presenting my first keynote, eeek. Even more terrifying, there are some really fantastic speakers there, including at least two of my mentors.

22 Nov: NoSQL Matters, Barcelona - presenting the AngularJS/Java/MongoDB demo again, with more of a focus on the MongoDB side. It’s going to be really nice to present so close to home!

I currently have nothing planned for 2015, and looking at that list of fixtures, I think I’d like it to stay that way for a while.
